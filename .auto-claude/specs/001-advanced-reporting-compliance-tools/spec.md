# Advanced Reporting & Compliance Tools

Comprehensive reporting suite including seasonal summaries, compliance dashboards, trend analysis, and regulatory document generation. Includes scheduled report generation and email delivery.

## Rationale
Professional reporting capabilities differentiate from Excel (pain-6-5, pain-6-6) while avoiding complexity that plagues all-in-one solutions (pain-2-2). Focused reports for hunting-specific needs rather than generic business intelligence.

## User Stories
- As a Vorstand, I want automatic monthly reports emailed to me
- As an Obmann, I want compliance dashboards showing my group's status
- As a regulatory liaison, I want professional reports for authority presentations

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Seasonal summary report generator
- [ ] Compliance status dashboard
- [ ] Scheduled weekly/monthly reports via email
- [ ] Custom date range reporting
- [ ] PDF export with professional formatting
